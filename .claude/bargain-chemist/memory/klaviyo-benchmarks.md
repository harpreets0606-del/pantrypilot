## NZ-SPECIFIC (must respect these)

### Privacy Act 2020 + IPP 3A (effective 1 May 2026)
- Unsolicited Electronic Messages Act 2007 — consent required.
- Direct Marketing Code — opt-out facility required.
- **NEW (May 2026):** Notify individuals when personal info collected from sources other than themselves. Affects how lead lists, integrations, and Meta Lead Ads are handled.

### BNPL adoption — must support at checkout
- Beauty/lifestyle = ~30% of transactions via BNPL
- Afterpay (1.2M+ active NZ users), Laybuy, Klarna, OpenPay
- ~25% of NZ adult population uses Afterpay

### Mobile dominance
- 65.7% of NZ ecommerce revenue from mobile
- 80%+ of NZ shoppers browse/buy on mobile
- Email creative must be mobile-first; test iOS Mail + Gmail Mobile

---

## RECENT MARKET SHIFTS

- **iOS MPP (since 2021):** Apple opens are auto-counted, inflating open rates. Use CTOR or click-rate as truer engagement.
- **Gmail Bulk Sender Rules (Feb 2024):** SPF/DKIM/DMARC enforced. One-click unsubscribe required. Spam complaint rate must stay <0.3% (warning at 0.1%).
- **List growth deceleration:** Annual list loss is 16% before any growth. Acquisition treadmill is steeper.
- **Flow > campaign:** Flows now drive 41% of email revenue from 5.3% of sends.
